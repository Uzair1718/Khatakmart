# **App Name**: KhattakMart Online

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse and view available grocery products (dry, frozen, milk) with images, descriptions, prices (PKR), and expiry dates.
- Shopping Cart: Add products to a cart, modify quantities, and view the order summary. Option to proceed to checkout.
- Checkout Process: Simple checkout process with options for Cash on Delivery (default) and card payment placeholder.
- WhatsApp Order: Automatic transfer of customer and order data (name, number, address, items, total, payment method) to a specified WhatsApp number to serve as a notification to KhattakMart.
- Order Confirmation & Notifications: Display a success message upon order placement and show order confirmation details on the website.
- Admin Dashboard: Secure admin dashboard with login. Manage products, view orders, and track payment & fulfillment status.
- Payment Verification: For card payments, allow users to upload a payment proof screenshot for verification and mark order as "Paid" by admin.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, yet inviting green (#3CB371) to reflect freshness and reliability, connecting with the grocery shopping experience. Avoids the clich√© of using green just for money, but evokes health and natural connotations.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated off-white (#F5F5DC). Provides a clean, calming backdrop that ensures focus on products. Consistent hue as the primary.
- Accent color: Earthy yellow (#B8860B) for highlighting important elements and call-to-action buttons. Its a distinct departure from the primary color in both brightness and saturation, but stays close to green on the color wheel.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a modern serif for headlines and titles, adding elegance.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', A clean and readable sans-serif for body text and product descriptions.
- Use clear and simple icons to represent product categories, shopping cart actions, and payment methods.
- A grid-based layout for product listings, a clean header navigation, and a single-column checkout process.
- Subtle animations when adding items to the cart or updating order status.
